Issuance of Wildlife Special Use Permit (WSUP) (Pursuant to Republic Act No. 9147 and PCSD Administrative Order No.05, as amended)

A Wildlife Special Use Permit (WSUP) for reef-fish-for food (RFF) species is issued ONLY to those entity involved in the business of buying and/or selling marine/aquatic (live or fresh) products, particularly Suno, Loba/Green Grouper, Lapung Baboy, in Palawan. It is a regulatory mechanism of the PCSD to ensure the sustainability of the RFF species.

Requirements.

  • Copy of Mayors Permit
    • - If the applicant is a corporation, partnership, cooperative or association, the corresponding document (i.e. articles of incorporation) showing that 60 % of their capital is owned by Filipinos must be submitted
  • Copy of Written commitment
  • ECAN Board Endorsement proof of application
