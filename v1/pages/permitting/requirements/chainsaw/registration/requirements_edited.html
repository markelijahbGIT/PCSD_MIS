Issuance of Chainsaw Registration (Pursuant to Republic Act No. 9175 and PCSD Administrative Order No.07, as amended)

The service may be availed by any qualified person who intends to secure Certificate of Registration (COR) for their chainsaw unit and be used in the province of Palawan. Provided, that only person who possess the qualifications enshrined in PCSD Admin Order No. 7 (Sec) 8.1.

The COR shall be valid for two (2) years upon issuance, renewable for the same period for those issued to individual or entities whose line of business or work are related to wood and/or timber production, processing and trade.

The COR shall be valid for five (5) years upon issuance, for those issued to non-commercial orchard and fruit tree farmers, and similar individuals or entities whose line of business or work are not related to wood and/or timber production, processing and trade. Renewal shall be of the same period, unless otherwise revoked by the issuing authority.

Provided further, that in cases where the qualification of registered owners is based upon any permit/instrument issued by other agencies (ex. CBFM, PLTP, etc.), the revocation/expiration of such permit/instrument shall automatically cause the revocation of the COR.

Who may avail:

  • Those who have secured Permit to Purchase and bought the chainsaw from authorized /registered dealer or from registered chainsaw owner (New)
  • Those who still have the qualification to possess/renew the Certificate of Registration
  • Receiver of donated chainsaw
  • Heir to the decedent by succession

REQUIREMENTS

For New Application for Purchase

  • Permit to Purchase
  • Official Receipt of the purchased chainsaw from the authorized dealer or Deed of Sale if bought from the private registered owner
  • Permit to Sell and Certificate of Registration, if bought from registered-owner
  • Stencil of serial number

For New Application for Donation

  • Permit to Purchase
  • Permit to Transfer by Donation
  • Stencil of serial number

For Renewal (Holder/s of Production Sharing Agreement, Co-Production Sharing Agreement, PLTP, SPLTP, CBFMA, IFMA, SIFMA, PCA issued permits, and other similar tenurial instruments)

  • Copy of the Certificate of Registration
  • Tenurial instrument in the name of the applicant or an endorsement from the PCA if the use is for coconut trees
  • Stencil of serial number

For Renewal (Orchard or Fruit Tree Farmer)

  • Certificate of Registration
  • Certificate of tree plantation ownership, or similar tenurial instrument
  • Certification from Barangay Captain concerned that applicant is an orchard or tree farmer
  • Original Certificate of Title (OCT), Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT), other documents that can prove ownership or legitimate possession of a piece of agricultural land
  • Stencil of serial number

For Renewal (Industrial Tree Farmer)

  • Copy of the Certificate of Registration
  • Certificate of tree plantation or similar instrument or Tax Declaration
  • Certification from Barangay Captain that applicant is an industrial tree farmer
  • Original Certificate of title/transfer certificate of title or other documents that can prove ownership or legitimate possession of land
  • Stencil of serial number

For Renewal (Agencies of the government, such as but not limited to LGU, Protected Area Offices (PAO), GOCCs that use chainsaws in some aspects of their functions)

  • Certificate of Registration
  • Certification from Head of Office or his/her authorized representative that chainsaws are owned/possessed by the office and used for legal purpose (specify)
  • Stencil of serial number

For Renewal (Other persons/entities that can show satisfactory proof that the possession and/or use of chainsaws is for a legal purpose)

  • Certificate of Registration
  • Affidavit that the chainsaw will be used for personal and for a legal purpose
  • Proof of ownership of chainsaw (sales invoice, deed of sale, deed of donation, through succession, etc.)
  • Stencil of serial number
  • Barangay certificate
  • Copy of Original Certificate of Title, Transfer Certificate of Title, other documents that can prove ownership or legitimate possession of a piece of agricultural land

Issuance of Permit to Transfer Certificate of Registration by Succession (Pursuant to Republic Act No. 9175 and PCSD Administrative Order No.07, as amended)

A Permit to Transfer Certificate of Registration by Succession is a permit being issued by PCSD to the heir of a deceased registered chainsaw owner. Provided, however that the heir has the qualification to possess/own a chainsaw unit

Who may avail: An heir to the decedent who is qualified to own/possess a chainsaw

REQUIREMENTS

  • Copy of tenurial instrument in the name of the applicant or an endorsement from the PCA if the use is for coconut trees
  • Certificate of Registration of the Donor
  • Permit Fee (Official receipt, payment/deposit slip)
  • Proof of death of the registered owner or the decent, such as Death Certificate
  • Certificate of Registration of the Decedent
  • Stencil of Engine Serial Number
  • Proof of succession by the applicant, such as extrajudicial settlement or its equivalent
